SmashPot
Mini-Golf for archers

Description

Draw, take aim and fire.

⁃ The game that transforms your phone into one of history's most recognizable weapons.
⁃ Literally, your phone is the bow!
⁃ Unique motion controls allow you to move your phone around to aim.
⁃ Beat levels by breaking all the pots without running out of arrows.
⁃ Sounds simple enough right? Just wait until you see all the puzzles waiting to be solved.
⁃ Requires pinpoint accuracy, reflexes and an understanding of quantum mechanics.
⁃ Test your skills and explore different environments.

This game is continually evolving, with every update we plan on adding more levels!If you want to help shape the games future send us your ideas!!
Thank you for playing!
